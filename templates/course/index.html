About Us

At EduSpark, we are on a mission to revolutionize education methods and empower learners to reach their full potential.

EduSpark is an innovative education technology company at the forefront of transforming the learning landscape through the fusion of gamification and artificial intelligence (AI). Our mission is to cultivate captivating and interactive learning experiences that ignite student inspiration and fuel their educational achievements.

Designed for kids

Our learning solutions, adapted to young pupils' learning needs, provide a dynamic and interactive educational experience that fosters their curiosity, engagement, and growth

Easy to use for teachers

Our AI-powered learning platform simplifies the process for teachers, enabling them to effortlessly deliver engaging and customized learning materials to their students
